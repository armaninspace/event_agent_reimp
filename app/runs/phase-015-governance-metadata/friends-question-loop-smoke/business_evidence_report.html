This report summarizes exploratory event-evidence workflow decisions. It preserves caveats and does not claim causal proof.

Selected candidate: turn-01-crowd-spending

Public rationale: Business owners need to know whether high-attendance weeks show measurable spending differences.

Caveat: Observational data cannot prove that crowds caused spending changes.

Evidence strength: exploratory workflow evidence; not causal proof.
